Interesting facts about Teddy Bears :-

In order to enter The Guinness Book of World Records, Lynn Lumb of Halifax of England made the smallest teddy bear measuring on 8.5mm tall.

The first teddy bear ever made and sold commercially was in the November of 1902 by a Brooklyn shopkeeper, Morris Michtom.

The 9th of September is celebrated as Teddy Bear day.

A teddy bear collector is called an ‘Arctophile’.

Interesting facts about Pandas :-

Pandas are good at climbing trees and can also swim.

Pandas go from pink to white and black (or brown).

Pandas are "lazy" — eating and sleeping make their day.

An adult can eat 12–38 kilos of bamboo per day!

Pandas have 6 toes to grasp bamboo.

You can see panda babies in August. They are usually born in August

Pigeons are incredibly complex and intelligent animals.
